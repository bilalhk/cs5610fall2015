Have you ever wondered who would win in a fight between Tom Cruise and Tom Hanks? Matt Damon and Will Smith? Charlize Theron and Michael Cera? Ye Old Arena! is an online 2 player game that allows you to pit famous celebrities against each other in epic turn-based combat. Just type in the name of a celebrity and we will create a character for you to use, and put you in a match against another player.
